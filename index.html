about me

let's talk allen.mcafee@gmail.com

I'm Allen McAfee. For the past 3+ years I've worked at Robo 3D as a VP of Engineering , making robots that make things. During that time I have had the honor to work directly with the great folks from Sony, Autodesk, Apple, NASA, MIT, and many more.

I believe in design thinking, agile and lean methodologies, and copious amounts of testing. I plan using SCRUM, task using Kanban, and lead by example.

I'm currently seeking new opportunities and challenges, where I can collaborate on bringing new products to market.

My idea of great product design combines a user centered approach with a strong and appealing design language. With the experience I gained as an engineering/product manager working directly with in-house and contracted developers, I am able to quickly adapt to challenges and recognize risks early on. As a developer, I enjoy getting involved in the whole process, starting from definition to designing, prototyping, testing, and shipping the ideas. My ideal work environment consists of a collaborative multidisciplinary team, that shares my love for details and reliability.
